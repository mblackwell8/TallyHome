How does TallyHome work?

  1. You enter the location and value of your house under the settings screen
  2. TallyHome collects the best house prices indexes for your location. These track how house prices have changed historically for your city, state or country. TallyHome automatically chooses the best index or combination of indexes to suit your house (although you can adjust this in the advanced settings, if you choose to)
  3. TallyHome calculates an estimated current value using the recent price trend

Tips

  1. Enter the most relevant geography: TallyHome tracks price indexes at the city, state and country level. If your house is in a big city, more than likely we have an index that is perfect for your situation. If you live in a regional or rural area TallyHome may rely upon the average of a broader set of indexes - nearby cities, the entire state etc. We think that you'll get the best result if you simply enter the closest city or the state or country you live in, but if you think another city is better, choose that one.
  2. Enter the most recent price or valuation: Clearly some suburbs or towns experience price increases that are higher than others. TallyHome works best with a fairly recent valuation or price, so if you bought your house a while back, enter your best guess at its current or recent value, not the price you paid. Alternatively, a real estate agent or home valuer may be able to provide a current estimate.
  3. Use the advanced settings: TallyHome has advanced settings that provide control over the indexes we include and the forecasting basis. You can change the defaults, which may improve the result for your specific circumstances.
